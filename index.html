We choose the theme of square dance to express the social environment and mental state of Chinese dama, or even dadis, who are different from us young people. Therefore, we went out of the house and went to the square to shoot and communicate, and felt the social world centered on square dancing and the illusion of returning to youth brought by this social world. Our work combines square dancing with youthful energy to show the different social world in the square we are exploring.

我们选取广场舞这一主题，通过广场舞来表达中国的大妈们，甚至是大爷，他们同我们年轻人不一样的社交环境与精神状态。为此我们走出家门，走向广场去拍摄与交流，感受大妈大爷以广场舞为中心的社交世界，以及这一社交世界带来的重回年轻的幻象体验。我们的作品让广场舞与活力年轻相结合，借此展现我们在广场上挖掘到的不一样的社交世界。
